WHAT IS SECURELY?

Securely is an AI-powered cybersecurity monitoring application that protects you from phishing attacks, malware, and malicious URLs in real-time. Unlike traditional security tools that only protect files or networks, Securely operates at the visual layer - monitoring everything that appears on your screen before you can interact with it.

Using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology and machine learning, Securely continuously scans your screen, extracts visible text, detects URLs, and analyzes them for threats. When a malicious link or suspicious pattern is detected, you receive an instant alert through notifications, sounds, and in-app toasts.

The application features a comprehensive dashboard with Quick Scan, 15-minute scan options, and continuous monitoring capabilities. All detected threats are logged in an audit log with screenshots and detailed summaries for review.

FEATURES

CONTINUOUS MONITORING

Runs in the background, continuously monitoring your screen for threats. Automatically starts when enabled and runs until you stop it. Zero-click protection that works across all applications.

QUICK SCAN

Instant on-demand scanning with a single click. Captures your current screen, performs OCR extraction, and analyzes with AI for immediate threat detection results.

15-MINUTE SCAN

Automated scanning mode that performs repeated scans every few seconds for 15 minutes. Perfect for monitoring during high-risk activities or when you need extended protection.

AI-POWERED ANALYSIS

Leverages Google Gemini AI for intelligent threat detection. Advanced machine learning models analyze text patterns, URLs, and content to identify phishing, malware, and security threats with high accuracy.

MULTI-LAYER ALERTS

Comprehensive alert system including Windows notifications, system sounds, and in-app toasts. Configurable alert preferences including notification settings, sound alerts, and visual indicators.

AUDIT LOG

Complete threat history with screenshots and detailed summaries. Review all detected threats with timestamps, analysis results, and visual evidence. Export and manage your security audit trail.

CUSTOMIZABLE SETTINGS

Flexible configuration options including notification preferences, hitbox overlays, sound alerts, strict mode, and test mode. Adjust monitoring sensitivity and behavior to match your needs.

PRIVACY-FIRST

Local processing with optional AI analysis. Your screen data is processed locally with OCR, and only analyzed text is sent to AI services. Complete control over your data and privacy.

HOW IT WORKS

STEP 01
SCREENSHOT CAPTURE
Continuously captures your screen using advanced screen capture technology. Monitors everything in real-time before you interact.
STEP 02
OCR TEXT EXTRACTION
Uses Tesseract OCR to extract all visible text from your screen. Converts visual content into analyzable text data.
STEP 03
KEYWORD SCANNING
Scans extracted text for suspicious keywords and patterns. Identifies potential threats through pattern recognition.
STEP 04
AI ANALYSIS
Sends data to Google Gemini AI for intelligent threat detection and analysis. Advanced machine learning evaluates threats.
STEP 05
THREAT DETECTION
Identifies phishing attempts, malicious URLs, and security threats. Determines risk level and threat type.
STEP 06
INSTANT ALERTS
Sends Windows notifications, plays alert sounds, and displays in-app warnings. Multi-layer alert system ensures you're notified.
STEP 07
AUDIT LOGGING
Automatically saves screenshots and detailed summaries of all detected threats. Complete security history for review.
STEP 08
DASHBOARD MONITORING
Real-time status updates and threat indicators in the application dashboard. Monitor your security at a glance.
